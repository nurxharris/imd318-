Primary School

Sekolah Kebangsaan Kampung Jawa 2 Klang, often abbreviated as SK Kampung Jawa 2 Klang, is a primary school located in Klang, Selangor, Malaysia. This school is part of the national education system, which follows the curriculum set by the Malaysian Ministry of Education.

SK Kampung Jawa 2, Klang

2010-2015

SK Kampung Jawa 2 Klang serves students from Year 1 to Year 6, typically aged between 7 and 12 years. The school aims to provide a comprehensive education that balances academic achievements with co-curricular activities to foster well-rounded individuals.

Secondary School

SMK Shahbandaraya is a well-regarded secondary school located in the bustling area of Klang, Selangor. The school is known for its commitment to academic excellence, holistic education, and fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for its students.

SMK Shahbandaraya, Klang

2016-2020

SMK Shahbandaraya places a strong emphasis on academic achievement, offering a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of subjects including sciences, mathematics, languages, and humanities. The school employs highly qualified teachers who are dedicated to delivering quality education and nurturing critical thinking skills. Regular assessments, enrichment programs, and extra classes are organized to ensure students excel in their examinations and are well-prepared for higher education.

School Uniform in Friday

Baju Melayu

Baju Melayu, the traditional Malay attire for men, has a special place in many Malaysian schools, including as part of the school uniform on Fridays. This practice is observed for various reasons, blending cultural heritage with modern educational settings.

School Sports Attire

Baju Sukan (Sports Uniform) in maroon color serves as a distinctive attire for sports events and physical education activities at SMK Shahbandaraya. The maroon color reflects the school's identity and instills a sense of pride and unity among students participating in sports activities.

University

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kedah is one of the campuses under the prestigious Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) system, which is renowned for its commitment to providing quality education and producing skilled professionals in various fields.

UiTM Cawangan Kedah, Merbok

2022-2024

UiTM Kedah is located in the northern region of Malaysia, specifically in the state of Kedah. The campus is situated in the vibrant town of Merbok, near Sungai Petani, providing students with access to both urban amenities and natural attractions. UiTM Kedah was established to cater to the educational needs of the northern region of Malaysia. It offers a wide range of academic programs to meet the diverse interests and career aspirations of its students.
