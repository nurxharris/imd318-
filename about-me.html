Early life.

Just a little kid in Klang.

February 14, 2003, I was born. I come from a family with six siblings, and I am the third child. And the photo on the right shows me as a youngster. I was born in Kuala Lumpur and grew up in Klang. When you look at the photo on this side, it seems like time has flown by. I didn't suppose this small guy had grown up to want to make his mother and father proud. Growing up, every milestone, every step forward, has been a testament to the love and support of my parents. That small, innocent face in the photo embodies the dreams and hopes of a bright future, dreams that have fueled my determination to succeed.

From a young age, I was curious and eager to learn, traits that were nurtured by my loving parents. They provided me with a supportive environment where education and hard work were highly valued. My parents' dedication and sacrifices were instrumental in fostering a strong sense of responsibility and ambition within me.

Reflecting on those early years, I realize how pivotal they were in shaping my path forward. The values instilled in me during my childhood have been the foundation of my journey, driving me to strive for excellence and to make my parents proud.

I love to play football.

Cristiano Ronaldo wannabe!!!

From a young age, I have been deeply passionate about football. This enthusiasm stems from growing up in a family that values sports in daily life. Take my father, for instance. In his younger years, he explored various sports such as football, hockey, and sepak takraw. Even now, approaching 60, he remains actively involved in tennis.

I began playing football at the age of 7 when my brother and his friends invited me to join their games. This is when my passion for the sport ignited. I also spent a lot of time watching my idol at the time, Cristiano Ronaldo, and admiring his playing style. He was playing for Manchester United then, a club I have loved ever since.

I sincerely wished that CR7 wouldn't transfer to Real Madrid at that time, but unfortunately, things didn't turn out the way I hoped.

I love photo shooting of myself & fashion.

Thrifting, capturing photos, trying something new.

I have always had a passion for photography, particularly capturing images of myself. There's something incredibly fulfilling about expressing myself through photos, experimenting with different poses, angles, and settings. Fashion, too, plays a significant role in this passion. I love exploring new styles, blending trends with my personal taste, and using fashion as a form of self-expression. Together, photography and fashion allow me to showcase my creativity and individuality, telling my unique story through every image.

As I mentioned earlier, I have a passion for fashion, and most of my clothes come from thrifting. I deeply love thrifting; there's an excitement in sifting through racks of pre-loved items and uncovering unique pieces with their own stories. Thrifting allows me to find stylish, distinctive clothes at a fraction of the cost, while also aligning with my commitment to sustainability. Each thrifted piece adds a unique touch to my wardrobe, seamlessly blending the past with the present in a way that's both fashionable and eco-friendly.

I love attending concerts.

The image on the left depicts me and a friend attending a concert in Penang featuring the Indonesian band Fourtwnty. The concert name was Nalar Tour Album. I initiated the invitation for us to go together because we share a mutual interest in the band. One of the main reasons I admire Fourtwnty is their music, which frequently incorporates calming melodies, heartfelt lyrics, and memorable hooks, resulting in a unique sound that connects with audiences.

The band gained popularity through their engaging live performances and the release of their debut album "Lelaku" in 2015, which featured hit singles like "Aku Tenang" and "Zona Nyaman". My Fashion Icon

Those below are among my references in fashion.

Hector Bellerin

Footballer

Hector Bellerin, the Spanish professional footballer known for his stylish off-field looks, has become a prominent figure in the fashion world. As a former defender for Arsenal FC, FC Barcelona and current for Real Betis Balompie, Bellerin's fashion sense extends beyond the pitch, often seen sporting trendy outfits at fashion events and on social media. He's known for experimenting with bold colors, unique patterns, and sustainable fashion choices, reflecting his passion for environmental activism. Bellerin's influence in the fashion industry continues to grow, earning him recognition as one of football's most stylish players.

Tyler the Creator

Rapper

Tyler, the Creator, is a multi-talented artist known for his unique sense of style both on and off the stage. As a rapper, singer, songwriter, and producer, Tyler's fashion choices often reflect his bold and eclectic personality. He's recognized for his unconventional outfits, which blend elements of streetwear, high fashion, and vintage pieces. Tyler isn't afraid to push boundaries with his fashion, experimenting with vibrant colors, unexpected patterns, and gender-fluid designs. His fearless approach to fashion has earned him praise from fashion critics and fans alike, cementing his status as a trendsetter in the industry.

Jackson Irvine

Footballer

Jackson Irvine, the Australian professional footballer, has garnered attention for his distinctive sense of style off the field. While primarily recognized for his skills on the pitch, Irvine's fashion choices often reflect a blend of casual sophistication and urban edge. He's frequently seen sporting tailored suits with unexpected twists, such as bold patterns or unconventional accessories. Irvine's fashion sense exudes confidence and individuality, showcasing his ability to effortlessly transition from the stadium to the streets with flair.
