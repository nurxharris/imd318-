    My Favourite Artists, Bands

    Those below are among my favourite artists and bands in music.

    Radiohead

    UK

    Radiohead is an English rock band formed in Abingdon, Oxfordshire, in 1985. The band consists of Thom Yorke (vocals, guitar, piano), Jonny Greenwood (lead guitar, keyboards, other instruments), Ed O'Brien (guitar, backing vocals), Colin Greenwood (bass), and Philip Selway (drums, percussion). Known for their experimental approach to music, Radiohead has influenced countless artists and remains one of the most critically acclaimed bands of their time.

    Baskara Putra

    Indonesia

    Baskara Putra, also known by his stage name Hindia, is an Indonesian musician, singer-songwriter, and producer. Born on February 8, 1990, in Jakarta, Indonesia, he is best known for his work as the lead vocalist of the band .Feast and for his successful solo career. Baskara is celebrated for his introspective lyrics, distinctive voice, and contributions to the Indonesian indie music scene.

    Noh Salleh

    Malaysia

    Noh Salleh, full name Mohammad Noh bin Salleh, is a prominent Malaysian musician, singer, and songwriter known for his work both as a solo artist and as the frontman of the influential Malaysian indie rock band, Hujan. Born on March 15, 1985, in Miri, Sarawak, Malaysia, Noh Salleh has made a significant impact on the Malaysian music scene with his unique voice and versatile musical talents.

    Rex Orange County

    UK

    Rex Orange County, born Alexander O'Connor on May 4, 1998, is an English singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist known for his heartfelt lyrics, eclectic sound, and unique vocal style. Emerging from the small village of Grayshott in Hampshire, England, Rex Orange County has quickly garnered a dedicated following and critical acclaim for his genre-blending music that incorporates elements of jazz, pop, and R&B.

    Oasis

    UK

    Oasis is a renowned English rock band that emerged in the early 1990s, playing a crucial role in the Britpop movement and achieving massive commercial success. The band was formed in Manchester in 1991 by guitarist and songwriter Noel Gallagher and his brother, lead vocalist Liam Gallagher. Known for their catchy melodies, anthemic sound, and the Gallagher brothers' volatile relationship, Oasis became one of the most influential and successful bands of the 1990s.
